FPAB FPAB The Family Planning Association of Bangladesh Bangladesh pioneered the family planning
movement in the country. The oldest and largest non-governmental organization (NGO) of its kind, FPAB
was established in 1953 under the dynamic of Prof. Dr. Humayra Sayed a renowned social workerwith
the objective of improving the quality of life of the underprivileged section of the society.
